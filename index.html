Work

Where to begin… I now have almost 10 years of customer service experience under my belt. I currently work for an awesome SaaS company called Simpleview here in the “Old Pueblo” Tucson, AZ. So who are we?

Simpleview is a software, data analytics and insights company that partners with destinations and their agencies to attract visitors, promote group travel and win bids for conventions and events.
Whether you market a resort, attraction, city, state or country, Simpleview has the tools and people to help you demonstrate your value and achieve your goals.

I started at Simpleview in 2018 as an Associate Support Manager for the CMS Support team. In that role I supported 150+ CMS/Web clients. Most recently I transitioned into my current role of Account Coordinator where I support an Account Director and Account Executive. I provide clients with any follow up needs they may have, as well as assisting his team members with day-to-day responsibilities in relation to individual and departmental goals.
